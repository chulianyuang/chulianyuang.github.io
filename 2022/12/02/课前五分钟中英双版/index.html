# The charm of Cantonese

Compared to Mandarin, Cantonese has more variability in tone. From a language point of view alone, such a language will make a good impression, so it is more layered into the song. Mainlanders prefer Cantonese songs because the language has a unique charm, and it will be more distinctive when added to the melody. Even if you can’t understand the meaning of the lyrics, you still cannot help humming along . Cantonese is loved by many audiences and fans.

Cantonese itself has a retro flavor. The reason why there is a retro taste is because the elements of ancient Chinese are still retained in the process of development. In order to adapt to the development of the times,many languages are becoming easier and simpler and some older words will be omitted or abandoned in the process, so that the language becomes more understandable. However, there are still some older words and pronunciations in Cantonese, which will add a historical atmosphere and cultural heritage to the language. Due to the existence of ancient Chinese elements, this language also has a more simple and elegant atmosphere, and adding melody and rhythm to Cantonese songs is more flavorful.

# 粤语的魅力

​ 相较于普通话而言，粤语在音调上有更多的抑扬顿挫。单从语言的角度来看，这样的一门语言会给人留下好听的印象，因此放入歌曲当中更有层次感受。内地人喜欢粤语歌，也是因为这一门语言具有独特的魅力，加上旋律之后会更加富有特色。即使不能够听懂歌词的含义，但是仍然会跟着哼唱也受到众多观众和乐迷的喜爱。

​ 粤语本身具有一种复古的味道。之所以会有复古的味道，是因为在发展的过程当中仍然保留了古汉语的元素。不少的语言为了适应时代的发展变得越来越简便，这个过程当中会省略或者放弃一些较为古老的词汇，让语言变得更加通俗易懂。但是在粤语当中仍然会有一些较为古老的词汇和发音，这就会这一门语言增加了历史的气息和文化的底蕴。由于古汉语元素的存在，也让这门语言有了更加古朴典雅的气息，放在粤语歌当中加上旋律和节奏就更加有味道。
